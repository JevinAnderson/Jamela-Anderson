My Nursing history

For the last 2 years or so, I've been a travelling nurse. I've worked in various units, hospitals, and States. In Arizona I worked at hospitals in Yuma and Phoenix. In Wyoming I worked in Cheyenne. In California I worked at hospitals in San Jose, Lafayette, and San Francisco. Before that, I worked primarily in Missouri at hospitals in Columbia and St. Charles.
I've worked in Ortho, Medical Surgical, and Telemetry units. I've filled in as charge nurse, mentored junior nurses, and learned from my colleagues. Traveling has given me confidence, a breadth of experience I think uncommon amongst my peers, and a quick ability to adapt to new environments.
