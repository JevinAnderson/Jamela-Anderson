This is me

My name is Jamela pronounced Juh - mee – lah.

I am a nurse with a bachelor degree in nursing and biology. I received my biology degree after 4 years a Mizzou. A week after graduating with that degree I entered a 15 month accelerated nursing program for a BSN. I have been a nurse for 4 years. I have done everything from acute rehab to PCU to travel nursing. Travel nursing was one of the greatest things I did in my nursing career. It allowed me to gain years of experience in just a few months. It also greatly increased my confidence as a nurse.
I am a newlywed bride as of December 2016. I married a very intelligent web developer who helped me build my own website you all get to enjoy. I am big on adventure. I enjoy fitness, hiking, traveling, and trying new things. My hobbies are forever changing. Right now they include surfing, rock climbing, bowling, and reading.

My Interests

"The Grand Canyon sucks, just rocks in a hole."
--Jamela Anderson

I like reading, especially when the books I read push me outside of my comfort zone. I enjoy it when I'm forced to reconsider my personal beliefs in light of what I'm reading. I also like to read about things I would never have thought about on my own. Some of the books I've enjoyed are: All the Beautiful and Ugly Things, The Devil All the Time, Big Little Lies, Firestarter, Holes, and Where the Hell is Tesla.
I love the great outdoors. I believe people were meant to be outside. I love anything that gets the heart pumping and the adrenaline flowing. I like snow-boarding, disc golfing, hiking, camping, canoeing, and rock climbing. I'd like to take up fishing and surfing.
The experience of traveling is something I've grown to love as well. I enjoy immersing myself in different cultures and seeing the beauty of new places. Some of my favorite places to visit are: Florence, Cozumel, Yosemite(The Grand Canyon sucks!), Rocky Mountains, Sarasota, Chicago, Rocky Point Mexico, Santa Cruz, and Yellowstone.
Finally, I like doing 'Happy Home Maker' activities. I like crochet, gardening, and cooking. I like putting healthy spins on classic dishes, but some of my best dishes are comfort foods. You have to try my chili, mashed potatoes, and fried chicken.
